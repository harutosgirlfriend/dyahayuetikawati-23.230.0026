about us

We have been established since 2011 with the idea of creating a comfortable place for introverts who enjoy solitude. With a very elegant concept, we can make visitors feel at home, enjoying a cup of coffee and bread for a long time
We offer healthy food that does not contain preservatives because the health of our customers is our top priority. We hope to serve food according to our customers' tastes and are ready to accept complaints if there are any mistakes in the menu served.
